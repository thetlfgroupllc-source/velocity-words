Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Velocity Words ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our speed reading web application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Account credentials
  • Communication preferences

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use Velocity Words:

  • Reading statistics (words per minute, time spent reading, documents read)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Usage patterns and preferences (theme selection, speed settings)

2.3 Document Content

When you upload or paste documents into Velocity Words, the content is processed in your browser for reading purposes. We do not permanently store your document content on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your payments and manage your subscription
  • Send you service-related communications and updates
  • Track your reading progress and statistics
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Document content is processed locally in your browser using localStorage
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
  • We use encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Account data is stored securely with access controls

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting our web application

These third parties have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected]

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use browser localStorage to store your reading preferences, statistics, and session information. This allows you to maintain your settings and track your progress. You can clear this data through your browser settings at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Velocity Words is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Velocity Words is operated from Orlando, Florida, United States. Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated. By using Velocity Words, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of Velocity Words after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: Consent and contract performance
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Data retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
