Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for Velocity Words subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider, Paddle.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds are provided at the sole discretion of Paddle and on a case-by-case basis and may be refused. Paddle will refuse a refund request if we find evidence of fraud, refund abuse, or other manipulative behavior that entitles Paddle to counterclaim the refund.

This does not affect your rights as a Consumer in relation to Products which are not as described, faulty or not fit for purpose.

3. Consumer Right to Cancel (EU/UK Customers)

If you are a Consumer resident in the European Union or United Kingdom purchasing for personal use, you have the right to cancel your purchase within 14 days without giving any reason.

The cancellation period will expire after 14 days from the day after completion of your purchase.

Exception for Digital Content: Your right to cancel does NOT apply once you have started downloading, streaming, or otherwise accessing our digital service. By using our service, you consent to immediate performance and acknowledge you lose cancellation rights once access begins.

To exercise your right to cancel, you must inform Paddle of your decision. Please contact Paddle directly through their customer support channels.

4. How to Request a Refund

All refund requests are processed by Paddle. Please contact Paddle directly for any refund inquiries.

You may also contact us at support@velowords.com for assistance, but final refund decisions are made by Paddle.

5. Subscription Plans & Cancellations

  • Monthly: $7.99/month
  • Quarterly: $23.97 every 3 months
  • Yearly: $95.88 per year

All subscriptions are managed and billed through Paddle. To cancel your subscription, use the Paddle customer portal link included in your purchase receipt or confirmation email.

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before your next billing date to prevent renewal.

6. Automatic Renewals

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless canceled through Paddle prior to renewal.

7. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback may result in immediate account suspension. Please contact Paddle support before filing a dispute to resolve billing issues.

8. Payment Processing

Paddle is the Merchant of Record for all transactions. Paddle handles all payment processing, tax collection, and refund administration.

For full terms and conditions regarding payments, refunds, and disputes, please refer to Paddle's Terms and Conditions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
