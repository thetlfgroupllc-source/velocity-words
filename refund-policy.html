Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains how refunds, cancellations, and billing disputes are handled for Velocity Words subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider, Paddle.

2. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Refunds, cancellations, and billing inquiries are handled through Paddle's systems.

3. Subscription Plans

  • Monthly: $8.99/month
  • Quarterly: $26.97 every 3 months
  • Yearly: $107.88 per year

Note: These are standard subscription prices. Special promotions or discounts may be available from time to time.

4. Refund Eligibility

4.1 Eligible for Refund

  • Verified billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Unauthorized charges (reported within 30 days)
  • Severe technical issues that prevent use of the Service and cannot be resolved within 5 business days

4.2 Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to cancel before renewal
  • Lack of usage during the subscription period
  • Partial billing periods
  • Performance expectations or reading speed results

5. How to Cancel Your Subscription

All subscriptions are managed and billed through Paddle.

To cancel your subscription:

  • Use the Paddle customer portal link included in your purchase receipt
  • Or use the subscription management link provided by Paddle in your confirmation email

Cancellations take effect immediately and prevent future renewals.

After cancellation:

  • No further charges will be made
  • You will retain access until the end of your current billing period
  • You will receive confirmation directly from Paddle

If you cannot locate your Paddle cancellation link, contact support@velowords.com and we'll assist you in locating it.

6. How to Request a Refund

Refund requests must be submitted via email to support@velowords.com and are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Please include in your refund request:

  • Your email address used for the account
  • Paddle invoice/receipt number
  • Reason for refund request
  • Date of purchase

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days. If approved, refunds are processed through Paddle back to your original payment method.

7. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed through Paddle
  • Funds are returned to the original payment method
  • Processing may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank/payment provider
  • Account access is revoked upon refund approval
  • No refunds for partial months or unused time

8. Automatic Renewals

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless canceled through Paddle prior to renewal. We send reminder emails 3 days before renewal.

9. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback may result in immediate account suspension. Please contact support before filing a dispute to resolve billing issues.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
