Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Velocity Words is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines our refund process for subscription services.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests are considered under the following circumstances:

Technical Compatibility Issues

If Velocity Words fails to function due to technical incompatibility with your primary browser that cannot be resolved through standard troubleshooting.

We strive to resolve technical issues before considering refunds. Most compatibility concerns can be addressed through our support channels.

2. Refund Request Process

2.1 Initial Support Contact

Before requesting a refund, please contact our support team to attempt resolution:

  • Submit a support ticket describing the issue
  • Allow 24 hours for initial response and troubleshooting guidance
  • Follow provided troubleshooting steps

2.2 Formal Refund Request

If the issue cannot be resolved, email support@velowords.com with:

  • Your account email
  • Brief description of the unresolved issue
  • Reference to your support ticket

3. Documentation Requirements

To verify technical issues, we may request:

  • Screenshot of any error messages
  • Browser name and version
  • Brief description of steps taken

Note: We aim to make this process straightforward while ensuring legitimate technical issues are properly documented.

4. Review and Processing

4.1 Review Timeline

Refund requests are reviewed in the order received:

  • Acknowledgment of receipt within 2 business days
  • Initial review within 3-5 business days
  • Additional information may be requested
  • Final determination communicated via email

4.2 Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The timeline depends on your payment method and financial institution.

5. Financial Terms

5.1 Refund Amount

Approved refunds are for the subscription amount paid, minus any third-party transaction fees that are non-refundable to us.

5.2 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. In some cases, we may offer platform credit as an alternative.

Processing Note: Some payment processors charge fees that cannot be recovered, which may affect the final refund amount.

6. Subscription Management

Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges but does not generate a refund for the current billing period. Refunds are separate considerations for service issues.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting Stripe support directly.

7. Digital Service Considerations

Velocity Words provides immediate access to digital speed reading tools upon subscription. This immediate delivery model is standard for digital services.

We encourage users to take advantage of our free resources and support channels to ensure the service meets their needs before extended use.

8. Discretionary Refunds

We reserve the right to issue refunds at our discretion in exceptional circumstances. Such exceptions do not establish precedent or alter this policy for other users.

Our goal is fair resolution of legitimate service issues while maintaining sustainable service operations.

9. Contact Information

For refund inquiries or technical support:

Email: support@velowords.com

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.

By subscribing to Velocity Words, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this refund policy. This policy is part of our Terms of Service.
