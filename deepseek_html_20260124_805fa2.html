Velocity Words is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Account preferences
  • Subscription status

1.2 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage information to improve our services:

  • Reading speed preferences
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Browser and device information

1.3 Document Content

Documents you upload are processed locally in your browser. We do not store document content on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process subscriptions through Stripe
  • Improve service functionality
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Communicate service updates

3. Payment Information

All payment information is processed securely by Stripe, Inc. We do not store or have access to your complete payment details. Stripe acts as our payment processor under their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Browser-based processing for document content
  • Secure account authentication

While we take reasonable precautions, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • GitHub Pages: Web hosting

These services have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

6. Data Retention

We retain account information while your account is active. Usage data is retained anonymously for service improvement. You may request account deletion by contacting support.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@velowords.com.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to maintain your reading preferences and session information. This allows personalized service without requiring repeated configuration.

9. Children's Privacy

Velocity Words is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Users

Velocity Words is accessible worldwide. Your information may be processed in the United States where our service infrastructure is located. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries:

Email: support@velowords.com

We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 3-5 business days.

This Privacy Policy is part of our Terms of Service. By using Velocity Words, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.
