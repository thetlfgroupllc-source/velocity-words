Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Overview

At Velocity Words, we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. This Refund Policy explains our refund and cancellation procedures for all subscription plans.

2. Subscription Plans

We offer the following subscription options:

  • Monthly: $9.99/month - billed monthly
  • Quarterly: $23.97 every 3 months ($7.99/month)
  • Yearly: $71.88 per year ($5.99/month)

3. Refund Eligibility

4.1 Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Technical Issues: If the Service experiences significant technical problems that prevent you from using it, and we cannot resolve the issue within 5 business days
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times for the same subscription
  • Unauthorized Charges: If your account was charged without your authorization (must be reported within 30 days)

3.2 NOT Eligible for Refund

Refunds are generally NOT provided for:

  • Change of mind after subscribing
  • Failure to use the Service during your subscription period
  • Forgetting to cancel before renewal
  • Dissatisfaction with reading speed results (individual results vary)
  • Partial month refunds (you retain access until the end of your billing period)
  • Previous billing periods if you've used the Service

4. How to Cancel Your Subscription

We make canceling simple - just email us and we'll take care of it:

Email support@velowords.com with "Cancel Subscription" in the subject line.

What happens when you cancel:

  • Your cancellation is processed immediately
  • You'll receive confirmation via email
  • No further charges will be made
  • Access ends when your cancellation is processed

5. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund based on the situations above:

  1. Email support@velowords.com
  2. Include "Refund Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your account email and reason for the refund
  4. Include any relevant details (billing dates, error messages, etc.)

We'll review your request within 24-48 hours and get back to you quickly.

6. Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed through Paddle to your original payment method
  • Processing typically takes 5-10 business days
  • The exact timing depends on your bank or card issuer
  • You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Your account access will be immediately terminated upon refund approval

7. Automatic Renewals

Your subscription automatically renews at the end of each billing period:

  • Monthly: Renews every 30 days
  • Quarterly: Renews every 90 days
  • Yearly: Renews every 365 days

You'll be charged automatically on renewal. To cancel anytime, just email support@velowords.com and we'll process it immediately - no waiting period required.

8. Failed Payments

If your payment fails:

  • We will attempt to charge your payment method up to 3 times
  • You will receive email notifications about failed payments
  • If all attempts fail, your subscription will be suspended
  • Your account data will be retained for 30 days
  • After 30 days, your account may be permanently deleted

No refunds are provided for periods when your account was suspended due to failed payments.

9. Promotional Pricing

If you subscribed during a promotional period (e.g., our 7-day sale), refund eligibility is the same as outlined above. Promotional pricing cannot be reinstated if you cancel and later re-subscribe.

10. Service Termination by Velocity Words

If we terminate the Service entirely:

  • We will provide at least 30 days notice
  • You will receive a prorated refund for any unused portion of your subscription
  • No refunds are provided if termination is due to violation of our Terms of Service

11. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or credit card company:

  • Your account will be immediately suspended
  • We will contest unauthorized chargebacks
  • You may be permanently banned from creating future accounts
  • Please contact us at support@velowords.com first to resolve any billing disputes

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

13. Florida Consumer Rights

As a Florida-based business, we comply with Florida consumer protection laws. Under Florida law, you have the right to:

  • Clear disclosure of automatic renewal terms before purchase
  • Easy cancellation of automatic renewals
  • Protection from deceptive trade practices
  • File complaints with the Florida Attorney General's Office if you believe your consumer rights have been violated

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits your rights under the Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act.
